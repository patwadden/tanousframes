Family Owned For Over a Century

The Tanous family have been in the framing business since 1905. We use the same skills and techniques that have been handed down in the family.

Over the past century we have been commissioned by artists of the eminence of Jacob Epstein, Augustus John, Nadia Benois, Sir Alfred Munnings and Vivian Pitchforth.

Our Frames

We make and restore traditional fine art frames using our unique collection of hand-carved, antique moulds. The Tanous family have been in the trade for over a century, and the team continue to use the same skills that have been handed down in the family for the last century.
